Brief Overview of ECON'24.

With participants and attendees coming from all across Pakistan and the expected audience to be around 1000, this mega event promises to be a melting pot of knowledge, creativity, and inspiration.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Some General Queries Answered.

    • Who can register for the event?

      Any undergraduate, college or school student can register for the event.

    • Will every participant get a certificate?

      Yes, even if you don't win anything, you will still get your participation certificate.

    • Can I transfer my registration to someone else?

      No, once registered you will have to be there or else the registration will be canceled with no refund.

    • How many modules can one typically take part in?

      If manageable, you can take part in multiple modules.

    • What happens if I want to change or drop a module?

      To change a module, you will have to directly contact the organizers. There will be no refund for dropping a module.

    • What are the accommodation facilities for the participants coming out of town?

      Participants can avail the accommodation facility for the whole event at the cost of Rs. 2000/- per head.

    • What are the nearby dining options for out-of-station participants looking for meals during ECON?

      Popular options available are Cheezious, NY212, and some local restaurants offering good food as well.
